About Me

James (he/him)

Hello!
My names is James and I am 31. My academic background is in Geology, but I have been working in a piano business since my early twenties. I have decided that it is time to close that chapter of my life and focus on changing to a career that is more aligned with my personal interests.


Why am I applying?

I would like to participate in Founders and Coders as I am looking for a change of career. Starting a new life-path in my 30s can be very daunting, but I am very eager to learn. I consider myself to be very adaptive and open-minded, and despite having no background in coding, I have made minor progress in learning the basics of programming in my free time using various resources provided by Founders and Coders. I have also tirelessly scoured the web for additional learning materials to help me get on track. Despite this, I am a firm believer in guided education and learning from professionals, which is why I would like to opt for a more organised approach to learning. I have had very limited time to complete the challenges as I currently work full-time, with many hours of overtime. All in all, I want to turn my life around and do something I really enjoy!
